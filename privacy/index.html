Privacy

How the SCU Foundation website handles logs, cookies, and third party services.

Scope

This privacy statement applies to the public website at scu.foundation and its subpaths, including institutional pages and the HTML edition of the SCU Whitepaper v1.8.

It does not apply to external sites linked from this domain, including the Lattice Protocol, the Lattice Research Institute, or the Post Silicon Collective.

Minimal Data Collection

The site is designed to operate with minimal data collection. Pages are served as static content and do not require registration, account creation, or the submission of personal information to access materials.

Server Logs

Standard technical logs may be generated by the hosting environment. These logs can include IP address, browser type, referring page, and timestamps. Logs are used solely for operational reliability, security monitoring, and abuse prevention. They are not used to build marketing profiles or behavioural datasets.

Cookies and Tracking

The site does not use analytics trackers, advertising pixels, behavioural profiling tools, or third party marketing scripts. If cookies are set, they are limited to essential technical functions such as load balancing or security.

Third Party Services

Some assets, such as fonts or embedded media, may be served from third party providers. These services may receive basic technical information from your browser when content is requested. Dependencies are kept minimal and chosen with stability, privacy, and long term accessibility in mind.

External Links

The SCU Foundation site may link to external resources or institutional partners, including the Lattice Protocol, the Lattice Research Institute, and the Post Silicon Collective.

This privacy statement does not apply to those external sites. You should review the privacy information provided by any third party service you choose to visit.

Contact

Questions about this privacy statement or the operation of this site can be directed to:

Email: contact@scu.foundation

Updates to This Page

This privacy page may be updated periodically to reflect changes in infrastructure, hosting, or operational practices. When material changes occur, the updated version will be published at this location.
