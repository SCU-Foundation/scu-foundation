Contact

The SCU Foundation welcomes enquiries related to the SCU architectural definition, the SCU Whitepaper, institutional publications, and the governed Lattice Protocol.

General Inquiries

For questions about the SCU architectural definition, the SCU Whitepaper, or institutional publications, please contact:

Email
contact@scu.foundation

The Foundation aims to respond to legitimate technical and institutional enquiries in a timely and professional manner.

Post Silicon Collective

For enquiries related to the Post Silicon Collective, collaborative research, or long horizon exploration, please contact:

Collective Email
we@p-sc.cc

The Collective operates independently of the Foundation's governance role and serves as a forum for open research and exploration.

Which contact should I use?

The SCU Foundation and the Post Silicon Collective serve different roles within the broader ecosystem:

  • contact@scu.foundation. For formal enquiries related to the SCU architectural definition, the SCU Whitepaper, publications, and institutional matters.
  • we@p-sc.cc. For Collective related enquiries, collaborative research, community discussions, and post silicon exploration.

If you are unsure which address to use, the Foundation will ensure your enquiry is routed appropriately.
