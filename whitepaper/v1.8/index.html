Abstract

The Superconducting Compute Unit (SCU) architectural definition outlines a post silicon computational model designed for cryogenic environments, ultra low energy switching, and high coherence digital logic. Version 1.8 consolidates the architectural framework, formalises system boundaries, and incorporates all updates required for the complete specification.
