SCU Whitepaper

The SCU Whitepaper defines the conceptual model, system structure, and long term direction of the Superconducting Compute Unit (SCU) architectural definition. The current canonical edition is SCU Whitepaper v1.8, with earlier editions, including v1.7, preserved for archival continuity. The Whitepaper is stewarded alongside the Lattice Protocol as part of the Foundation's open standards mandate.

Current Edition

SCU Whitepaper v1.8 is the current canonical edition of the SCU architectural definition. It supersedes v1.7 and incorporates all updates required for the complete specification.
