Security Disclosure

Guidelines for responsible disclosure of security related issues affecting SCU Foundation websites, publications, or infrastructure.

Responsible Disclosure

The SCU Foundation welcomes responsible reporting of security vulnerabilities. Reports are reviewed promptly and handled with professionalism, confidentiality, and respect for the reporter's time and effort.

This page applies to the public website at scu.foundation and its subpaths, including institutional publications and the HTML edition of the SCU Whitepaper v1.8.

It does not apply to external sites such as the Lattice Protocol, the Lattice Research Institute, or the Post Silicon Collective.

Contact

Security related issues may be reported via:

contact@scu.foundation

The Foundation does not operate a bug bounty program. Responsible disclosure is appreciated and contributes to the long term stability of the ecosystem.
